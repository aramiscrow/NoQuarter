Welcome To Animaze...ing: The Stories of Kevin Seymour.

aramis@animazeingthestoriesofkevinseymour.com

This project is dedicated to honor the legacy and memory of the

Director/Producer/Creator/Artist/Visionary Kevin Seymour and his company Animaze...inc.

This podcast (later a documentary) based on his work bringing anime to the west through firsthand industry interviews

and by doing in depth reviews and analysis of the dubbing process for all media properties covered under Animaze.

I will also be discussing ZRO Limit Productions, Magnitude 8 Post, and Shadow Box Studios.

If you are reading this and your career intersected with Kevin and his production company Animaze

it would be an honor to include your stories about him.
